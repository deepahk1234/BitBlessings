The Energy of Blessings

 ▼

In the realm of intention and energy, there exists a profound distinction that many fail to grasp.

A wish is merely a hope or a nice thought—a gentle whisper carried on the wind, lacking substance or force. A blessing, however, is fundamentally a piece of energy—tangible force, measurable potential, pure momentum.

This energy is available only if a person is fluid enough to receive it. When you are fluid, blessings flow into you like rain into fertile soil. But when you become crystallised—hardened by bitterness or fear—even the most powerful blessing cannot penetrate your defenses.

Which tree calls to you today? Are you fluid enough to receive its answer?
